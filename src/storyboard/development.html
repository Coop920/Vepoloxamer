Timeline Advantage — Established Manufacturing Process

A Known Process, Documented Specifications, and Global CDMO Accessibility

GMP manufacturing development for a novel drug typically requires 12–18 months and $2–5M+ in upfront investment before a single clinical dose can be manufactured. Vepoloxamer's manufacturing process has already been developed, implemented, and characterized — the analytical specifications are defined, the purification method is documented, and the process has been successfully run at commercial scale. A strategic acquirer can engage an established global CDMO with relevant polymer processing experience and reestablish the supply chain without the extensive process development investment typically required for a new chemical entity.

This translates directly to a compressed clinical startup timeline and lower upfront capital requirements compared to assets where manufacturing development remains to be done from scratch.
